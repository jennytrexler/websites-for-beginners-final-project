rats

what is a rat?
Merriam Webster defines rat as: any of numerous rodents (Rattus and related genera) differing from the related mice especially by considerably larger size. However, rats are much more than this. They have amazing memories and can even be trained to sniff out bombs. But, whatever your opinion, we just ask that you give them a chance, or even invite them in with some fruit and a wedge of cheese
