why rats
Rats are extremely social and affectionate animals. They enjoy the company of other rats and love being with humans too. When happy, rats have been observed to chatter or grind their teeth. Although very curious animals, rats are also shy, and prefer to run away than confront a potential threat. We may love rats becuase their behaviors closely resemble ours, but there are plenty more ways to appreciate rats!
making us happy
When happy, rats have been observed to chatter or grind their teeth. Rats make happy “laughter” sounds when they play.
like us, rats are ever-changing
According to multiple experts, rats left hungry amid food shortages during the novel coronavirus outbreak are turning to cannibalism to survive. Experts warn that the cannibalism could lead to a smarter, more aggressive rat breed in the future.
